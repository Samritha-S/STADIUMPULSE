StadiumPulse

AI-assisted crowd safety intelligence for FIFA World Cup 2026.
Below is the central portal for the final hackathon submission. Select a view to explore the live endpoints (powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash).

Control Room

Dashboard for stadium operators. Monitors live zone forecasting and receives automated Gemini situational briefs for watch/critical zones.

Fan Companion

Mobile web view for attendees. Receives personalized, multilingual routing nudges (with accessibility routing) and transit tips to bypass congestion.

Volunteer Triage

Field reporting tool. Volunteers submit free-text issues in any language, and Gemini instantly classifies location, severity, and category.
