Venue Information

Everything you need to know about MetLife Stadium for the World Cup Final

📍 Location & Access

  • Address: 1 MetLife Stadium Dr, East Rutherford, NJ 07073
  • Entrances: Gates A – H, clearly signposted from all car parks
  • Step-free access: All gates with dedicated accessible lanes
  • Drop-off: Zones marked Blue (accessible) and Yellow (standard)

🚇 Getting Here

  • NJ Transit: Direct trains from New York Penn Station
  • Bus: Express services from Port Authority Bus Terminal
  • Driving: I-95, NJ Turnpike Exit 16W · Parking from Lot A–G
  • Rideshare: Designated pickup/drop-off at Lot G entrance

🎫 Match Day Essentials

  • Gates open: 3 hours before kick-off
  • Bag policy: Clear bags only (12" × 6" × 12" max)
  • Prohibited: Outside food/drinks, umbrellas, noisemakers
  • Permitted: Factory-sealed water (<1 litre), medical equipment

🌿 Sustainability at the Cup

  • 100% renewable energy powering stadium operations
  • Zero-waste concessions with compostable packaging
  • Electric shuttle fleet — zero direct emissions
  • Carbon offset programme for every ticket holder
